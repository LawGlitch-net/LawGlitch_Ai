What Is Mira?

Mira is not another chatbot. It's a persistent, always-on AI copilot that lives inside your browser as a floating overlay. Tailored for professionals, researchers, and power users, Mira bridges the gap between complex task execution and intuitive user control — integrating voice commands, automations, and advanced screen content capture directly into your workflow.

Self-Learning AI

As you work, Mira's AI continually refines its performance — becoming faster, cheaper, and smarter with each interaction. A completely isolated instance per user guarantees privacy and data security.

User-Gated Capture

The User-Gated Capture Copilot system facilitates high-fidelity data acquisition through explicit user control. Browser-native tab picker dialogs guarantee consent — preventing background or unauthorized recording.

Core Capabilities

Every feature designed for precision, privacy, and real-world execution.

Persistent Floating Overlay

How It Works

From install to execution in five steps.

01

Install & Initialize

Install Mira from your browser's extension store. Refresh open tabs to activate the persistent overlay across your entire browsing experience.

02

Configure Your Backend

Launch the floating sidebar and input your secure configuration — Supabase URL, anon key, and AI Chat Webhook. Contact sales@lawglitch.in for credentials.

03

Start Working

Chat by typing or speaking. Attach files, reference page content, capture screens with OCR. Mira understands your browser context and acts on it.

04

Automate & Capture

Activate the automation engine, start screen sharing via the browser's tab picker, and click to capture frames. Voice commands trigger actions without interrupting flow.

05

Review & Export

End capture sessions to upload results to your isolated backend. All chat histories persist locally. Navigate, refine, and repeat.
